Know your cluster points. Before you have to decide.

Enter the KCSE grades you expect (or already got). The calculator uses the standard KUCCPS weighted cluster-points formula and tells you which degree programmes you are competitive for, given 2023 placement cutoffs.

This is an estimate. KUCCPS does not publish the exact cluster-point formula. This calculator uses the commonly-circulated approximation C = sqrt((r/R) * (t/T)) * 48. The official value uses confidential Performance Indices computed by KNEC and may differ by a point or two. The 2023 cutoffs below come directly from the KUCCPS 2024/2025 Degree Programme Cutoffs PDF. Always verify with kuccps.ac.ke before making decisions.
